### The Methodology
Our internal tool at **AiAuditScan** analyzed specific technical signals across 409 brands.

| Metric | Findings | Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JSON-LD Depth** | 1.7% with Product Schema | Critical invisibility |

### Finding #1: The JSON-LD Illusion
The high-level data is deceptive. While 40.3% of sites use some form of JSON-LD, the depth is non-existent. **Only 1.7% of the 409 sites scanned have Product schema.**

98.3% of brands are missing the specific markup required for product discovery. Having Organization schema without Product schema is a strategic error. When a user asks an AI to compare your brand, the AI sees you exist but cannot extract the data to recommend you.

### Finding #2: A State of Total Invisibility
The distribution shows a polarized market:
* **56.7%** scored below 20/100 (Technically invisible).
* **35.2%** scored between 40-60/100 (Partial visibility).
* **Only 0.7%** scored above 80/100.

### Finding #3: The Platform Effect
* **Shoes (31.8/100):** High Shopify Plus adoption leads to better defaults.
* **Beauty (22.0/100):** Premium brands on legacy systems suffer.
* **General Retail (8.3/100):** The worst performers overall.
